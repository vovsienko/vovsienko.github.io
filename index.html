About Me

Hi, my name is Viktoriia. I was born and lived most of my life in Donetsk, Ukraine. When the War came to my home I became an IDP (Internally Displaced Person) as we're many others from Donetsk and Lugansk. As a result, I moved to Dnepropetrovsk where I live now. As we all know, every coin has two sides and although I've lost my home and most of my career, I also met new people and was presented new opportunities. I was lucky enough to be allowed to enroll in a special course for Front End Development organized by the UNDP and sponsored by the Government of Japan. The course was designed to help IDPS to gain new knowledge, skills and future job opportunities.

On March 30, 2016, I successfully graduated and now am now looking for an Internship or Trainee position to start my way in Web Development. I have many years of expereinece in Import, Logistics as well as Administration. I like to help people and now I work as an Admin Fin/HR Assistant at Médecins Sans Frontières (MSF). At the same time I would like to change my professional objectives and specialize in Frontend Development. I love working with passionate people and sharing what I've learned with them. This website is my window to the world where you can find information about me and then get in touch to communicate about potential projects.
